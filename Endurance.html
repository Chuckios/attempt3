Here's the gear we used

  • Black Diamond Gridlock Magnetron Carabiner
  • Two 40m ropes
  • ~15 quick draws (Petzl Spirit and DMM Alpha Sport
  • Gri Gri 2
  • ATC
  • Helmet
  • Personal anchor system
  • Lots of snacks and water

Person A

  1. Get some draws, and tie the figure eight on a bight
  2. Lead your first lap on the route
  3. Once at the top, lower
  4. Once on the ground, start climbing immediately
  5. Once at the top, lower
  6. Once on the ground, clip the carabiner to your partner's belay loop. Pull the rope and put your partner on belay
  7. Belay and lower
  8. When the climber is on the ground, take him/her off belay and pull the rope. When the rope falls down, put the climber on belay and say "on belay"
  9. Once your partner complete the 2nd lap, clean the route
  10. Once your partner is on the ground, remove the belay, pull the rope, and put the belay back on
  11. Repeat steps

Person B

  1. Flake the rope
  2. Belay
  3. Lower the climber
  4. When the climber is on the ground, take him/her off belay and pull the rope. When the rope falls down, put the climber on belay and say "on belay"
  5. Lower the climber.
  6. Once the climber is on the ground, take him/her off start climbing
  7. Clip the chains and lower
  8. Once on the ground, start climbing immediately
  9. Finish your 2nd lap and clean the route
  10. Once you are on the ground, start the next route immediately
  11. Repeat steps

Notes

  • Prioritize safety, fun, and lastly speed.
  • You need to BE ON TOP OF SAFETY THINGS since the partner check is skipped and speed is emphasized.
  • You climb in blocks of 4 routes.
  • Always be doing something. If you have down time, drink water, have a snack, mentally prepare for the next route/area.
  • Try not to high clip. It uses extra time and energy holding onto the wall for longer while you clip.
  • Don't leave your GriGri at the previous wall and have to run back and get it.
